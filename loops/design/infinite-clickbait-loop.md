# The Infinite Clickbait Thumbnail Loop

**Category:** Design | **Author:** @Alex_FF
**Published:** 2026-06-21 | **Source:** https://signals.forwardfuture.ai/loop-library/loops/infinite-clickbait-loop/

## Use When

You need to create high-performing YouTube thumbnails through iterative scoring and improvement.

## Prompt

> For [video], use [approved assets] to make ten thumbnail concepts. Score each at real YouTube sizes against [inspiration channel] for clarity, curiosity, emotional pull, contrast, and accuracy. Take the top three, improve each one's weakest dimension, and rescore them under the same rubric. Keep iterating the strongest concept until it clears [quality threshold] or [budget] ends. Reject anything the video cannot deliver.

## Verification

- **Title:** One accurate thumbnail clears the fixed quality threshold.
- **Detail:** Winner outscores alternatives under same conditions.

## Steps

1. Define the video subject, approved assets, inspiration channel, quality threshold, budget, five-part rubric.
2. Create ten distinct concepts, inspect at real YouTube sizes, score each.
3. Select top three, improve weakest dimension of each, rescore.
4. Stop at quality bar or budget, reject misleading concepts.

## Why

Varied first set creates real options. Fixed rubric makes later rounds comparable.
