# The Artifact-to-Skill Loop

**Category:** Evaluation | **Author:** Hiten Shah (@hnshah)
**Published:** 2026-06-21 | **Source:** https://signals.forwardfuture.ai/loop-library/loops/artifact-to-skill-loop/

## Use When

You need to extract reusable methods from successful artifacts.

## Prompt

> Turn [artifact] into a skill, playbook, or procedure. Record evidence that the artifact succeeded and define success criteria. Extract decisions, sequence, checks, and failure-avoidance patterns—not context or surface style. Remove sensitive material. Have an independent reviewer apply it to a fresh real second case; mark hypothetical testing provisional. Revise at most twice. Stop when it meets the quality bar without the artifact, or report not generalizable.

## Verification

- **Title:** The extracted method succeeds on a fresh second case without the original artifact.
- **Detail:** Independent reviewer confirms the method works standalone.

## Steps

1. Confirm that the source artifact has credible evidence of success.
2. Separate the durable decisions, sequence, checks, standards from one-off facts.
3. Write the method as a standalone skill, playbook, or procedure.
4. Have an independent reviewer apply it to a fresh real case.

## Why

Strong outputs often get saved while the method disappears. Extracting makes knowledge reusable.
